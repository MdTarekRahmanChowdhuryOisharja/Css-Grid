RoyGas Apps

We have different web and mobile apps to manage gas stations. Station owners can subscribe one or more apps of their convenience. The applications can be run from both PC and mobile devices.

POS

RoyGas POS app all allows sales people to record gas sales and print receipts for the customers.

SALES & ACCOUNTING

Sales and Accounting app records daily salles summary,gas purchase, gas stock, petty cash and cash statements.

GAS STATION LOCATOR

Gas Station Locator app helps to locate and get directions to specific Fuel/Gas stations.
